Our Mission

At Motorola we have deep roots into bringing communication into the world,
and we strive to grow those roots into the future. We aim to never be stagnant
and to always be innovating. We are driven on providing the world with new

Motorola is a world leader in the telecommunications industry. Our history is full of innovations

we bring the worlds first mobile phone. With groundbreaking innovations, we have had a significant
influence on shaping the world of communication.
